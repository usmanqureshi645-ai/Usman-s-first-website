IFRS 9: Financial Instruments

IFRS 9 governs the recognition, measurement, and disclosure of financial instruments. It introduces the expected credit loss model for impairment, replaces the old incurred-loss model, and covers classification and measurement, hedge accounting, and derecognition. This hub brings together comprehensive guidance on financial instrument accounting.
