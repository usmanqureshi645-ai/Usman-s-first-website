## LinkedIn Posting Specs

### Optimal Image Size:
- **Vertical (recommended):** 1200×2640px (1:2.2 ratio)
- **Square alternative:** 1080×1080px (1:1)
- **Horizontal:** 1200×627px (16:9, less optimal for feed visibility)

### File Format:
- **PNG** (best for quality, larger file)
- **JPG** (smaller file, still high quality)
- **WebP** (compressed, good quality, not all browsers support)

### LinkedIn Upload Tips:
1. **Vertical posts get 46% more impressions** than horizontal.
2. Use **1:2.2 aspect ratio** for maximum mobile visibility.
3. **Avoid text in outer 10% edges** — LinkedIn crops slightly on mobile.
4. **Test on mobile preview** before posting — text readability matters.
5. **Color contrast:** Navy + gold on white = readable at all sizes ✅
