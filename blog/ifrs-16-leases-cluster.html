IFRS 16: Lease Accounting

IFRS 16 requires lessees to recognize a right-of-use asset and lease liability for almost all leases on the balance sheet. This fundamental change from IAS 17 affects financial statements, ratios, and disclosures. This hub brings together our complete lease accounting guidance: from lease identification and liability calculation to discount rate determination, journal entries, modifications, and audit testing.
