IAS 36: Impairment of Assets

IAS 36 requires entities to test assets for impairment when indicators suggest their carrying amount exceeds recoverable amount. Recoverable amount is the higher of fair value less costs and value-in-use. This hub covers CGU identification, impairment testing, and valuation techniques.
