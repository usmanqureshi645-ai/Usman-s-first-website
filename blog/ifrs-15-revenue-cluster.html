IFRS 15: Revenue Recognition

IFRS 15 standardizes revenue recognition across industries using a single five-step model: identify the contract, identify performance obligations, determine transaction price, allocate price, and recognize revenue as obligations are satisfied. This hub provides complete guidance on the standard that replaced seven legacy revenue standards.
