Why IFRS 18 Exists

The IASB introduced IFRS 18 to improve comparability, regulate Management Performance Measures and standardise presentation of financial performance.

Key Changes

  • Mandatory Operating Profit subtotal
  • Operating, Investing and Financing categories
  • Formal regulation of MPMs
  • Enhanced unusual item disclosures
  • Comparative restatements required

Who Is Most Impacted?

Banking, Technology, Media, Real Estate, Asset Management, Manufacturing and listed groups.

Readiness Roadmap

Gap Assessment → Policy Design → Systems Changes → Dry Run Comparatives → Go Live.

Learning Outcomes

Participants will be able to perform impact assessments, design MPM reconciliations, update policies and prepare IFRS 18 compliant reporting.
