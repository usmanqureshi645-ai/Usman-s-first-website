- **0:02:** FLASH — Gold light burst (like a camera flash or explosion).

  5. Dashboard cards flying in (parallax, depth, excitement).

- High contrast (blacks are crushed, whites blow out slightly — intentional for energy).

- Light trails following UI elements.

**Animation:**
- **Speed:** Everything happens FAST. No easing; snap transitions.
- **Parallax:** Each UI layer moves at different speed (foreground leading, background trailing).
- **Glitch effect (optional):** 1–2 frame glitch/jitter on scene transitions (adds digital energy, 20ms duration, very subtle).
- **Glow pulses:** Each element glows on appearance (gold @100% opacity, instant to 50%, rapid).

**Transition:**
- Hard cut (no fade) from each clip to next.
- **0:05:** Cut to full logo/text reveal.

**Voiceover:**
**(None — silence except music.)**

**On-screen text:**
**(None yet — visuals only, hook the eye.)**

**SFX:**
- **0:02:** Explosive whoosh (deep, punchy, -12dBFS).

**Emotional objective:** "WHOA. What IS that??" Grab attention in 3 seconds. Make viewers stop scrolling.

## MUSIC SPECIFICATION — HIGHLY ENERGETIC

**BPM:** 140–150 BPM (fast, energetic, modern).

**Structure:**
- **0:00–0:02:** Silence (builds anticipation).
- **0:02:** EXPLOSION — Massive orchestral hit. Kick drum + snare + strings + synth all at once.
- **0:02–0:08:** High-energy section. Trap-style drums (heavy kick, snappy snare). Bright synth stabs. Orchestral strings building in layers.
- **0:08–0:15:** Sustain energy while allowing voiceover clarity. Drums less prominent, pad synth enters. Strings swell. Less busy texture (so VO can land).
- **0:15–0:30:** Peak energy. Full orchestra. Drums driving hard. Synths bright. Building toward climax.
- **0:30–0:40:** Slight dip (accessibility section) — still energetic but warmer. Drums pull back. Strings sustain. Feels accomplished.
- **0:40–0:48:** Build toward final peak. Drums return stronger. Synths brighten. Emotional strings swell (triumph).
- **0:48–0:55:** Climax + resolution. All elements full. Warm, confident, powerful.
- **0:55–0:60:** Resolve to single held chord. Fades out smoothly.

**Instrumentation:**
- **Drums:** Trap/hip-hop style kick, snappy snare, hi-hat rolling. NO acoustic drums (too warm for opening energy). Synthetic, punchy.
- **Strings:** Lush, orchestral (violins, violas, cellos). Ascending patterns (building energy). Warm, emotional (not harsh).
- **Synths:** Bright, modern. Stabs for energy. Pads for sustain. Some pluck synth for percussive hits.
- **Bass:** Deep sub-bass, felt more than heard (adds power, -20Hz to 50Hz, felt in chest).
- **No vocals. No lyrics.**

**Emotional arc:** Explosive → Energetic → Building → Triumphant → Resolved.

**Reference:** 
- Stripe launch video music (modern, orchestral, energetic).
- Tesla announcement music (powerful, inspiring, tech-forward).
- Linear.app launch (cinematic, energetic, building).

**Platforms to find similar:**
- Epidemic Sound: search "high energy tech launch", "exciting corporate", "modern orchestral".
- Artlist: search "energetic startup", "powerful tech", "inspiring electronic".
- Musicbed: search "confident electronic", "triumphant orchestral".
- Or: hire a composer with brief: "140 BPM, orchestral + electronic fusion, energetic opening, warm resolve, inspiring and modern."
